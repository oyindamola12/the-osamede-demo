Patrick Doyle started out as a radio presenter
on the Voice Of Nigeria in 1980. He has since
remained in the media space in different roles and
capacities, evolving into being a TV personality with
landmark shows which include the hugely popular late
night talk show of the 90's " "FRIDAY NIGHT LIVE".
As a compere Patrick has handled many epoch making
national events as well as Award ceremonies. He also
veered into the motion picture industry as a leading actor,
screen writer, producer and director with numerous credits.
His iconic movie "THE GARDNER" set the bar for better
themed Nollywood movies in the mid 90's. His recent
movie "MADAM PRESIDENT" has equally elicited critical
acclaim as a political thriller. Patrick worked as
content development consultant for Silverbird TV and
is credited with creating the stations flagship
breakfast news show. In recent times he has produced
many TV documentaries on behalf of several state
governments and institutions as well as corporate
clients. Patrick operates as a media consultant
for politically exposed high profile individuals
and designs media engagement strategy for discerning
clients. Presently he is the host of a news analysis
show on TV " News Scope With Patrick Doyle" airing
on Silverbird TV and "Viewpoint" Rhythm FM's
flagship current affairs show.

Trained at The Creative Arts Department
of The University of Lagos. Patrick Diabuah
is a versatile actor well known for his ability
to adapt to any given role on stage and in front
of the camera. He is also a Sculptor, Voice actor
and voiceover artist, singer and acting coach.
He has been in the practice of professional acting
for theatre as well as film for over seven years.
His voice works include the First Bank @100 audio
documentary, 2018 Access Bank Lagos Marathon Radio
Advert, Genii Games' Children stories series.
He has featured in theatre productions like Lottery
Ticket, Our Husband Has Gone Mad Again, Kakadu
the musical, Saro the musical, Wakaa the musical,
Fela And The Kalakuta Queens, Sankara, The Trials
of Brother Jero, Fractures, Band Aid, Boney Manili,
Adio the musical, Our Son The Minister, Death and
the King's Horseman, Threesome, Beautiful wilderness,
Our Duke Has Gone Mad Again, and many more His
Tv/film credits include; Seven, God Calling,
Banana Island Ghost, 93 Days, Zone 222, B4 30,
24, E.V.E His Mother's Son, Seven,
God Calling, Binding Duty.

Olanrewaju Dele-Abrahams is an actor, director,
writer and producer with about 25 years of experience
in the arts and entertainment industry. He has also
worked in the education sector as an educator and Head
of School. He holds a certificate in Music (1997), B. A.
Degree in Creative Arts (2002), Master’s Degree in
Public and International Affairs (2011), Master’s Degree
in the Arts of the Theatre (2017); all from the prestigious
University of Lagos respectively and a PGD in education;
Nigeria Teachers’ Institute/University of Ilorin.
He is an unrepentant lover of his craft. He has worked
on several projects with notable directors and he has
a few self produced productions to his credit. His
current project 'Love and its O.S.' is billed
for a comeback in February 2022.

Academy trained screen Actor and a Toka
Mcbaror Actors Master Class Participant
with two years professional acting experience
and about five years unprofessional stage
acting experience. Following his professional
acting debut in 2019 in the Toka Mcbaror series
“Paperboat” as “Frank”, Boman has featured on
“Zubaida” , “Our Best friend’s wedding “, Baron’s
World Series “My Flatmates” as “Dayo” and most
recently as “Andrew” in the Ebonylife Creative
Academy(second intake 2021) short film “Waiting
For You” where he won the Award for Best Male
in a Supporting Role. Boman is Slim, 6ft tall,
brown skinned with black eyes. He is fit, weighs
about 70kg and lives in Lagos State. He speaks
English, Pidgin and Hausa languages fluently.
He is also a Singer and a Song writer, a
committed and reliable Individual with an
unwavering interest, passion and drive for
Acting. He loves to learn, meet people,
take chances and challenge himself in
his Acting. Boman cannot wait to work
with you on your next project.
