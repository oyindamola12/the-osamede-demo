Gold Lilies Global Services in collaboration with DeClassical Arts and Entertainment
Company presents a remarkable depiction of culture, women empowerment and
social justice in a phenomenal and musically infused stage play, Osamede!

OSAMEDE, meaning “Who the King has crowned” in the Benin language is a story
of an orphan girl, Osamede. Raised by her Uncle, she is chosen as a bride of the ruler
of the one of the largest provinces in Benin during a time of great economic development,
volatile political tension and severe oppression of her tribe. Facing the possibility of losing
her life and further endangering her people, she risks it all, leaning to courage over fear,
to change the lives of not just her community but that of generations to follow.

This intrigue filled and captivating stage play which is enacted through the ancient,
rich and classical Benin Kingdom with a thread of modernity, is an empowering body
of work and a strong call for our collective service to humanity that
will stir up hope and courage.
