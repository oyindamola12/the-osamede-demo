AYO AJAYI / Director / he is a theatre producer and director is a pioneer music
director in the Nigerian theatre industry who to date has directed well over 50
musicals producing some of Nigeria's finests. His active participation in the
Nigerian theatre space started when he directed the music of the popular
Saro the musical and Wakaa the musical (Lagos & London) both from
the stables of BAP Production.

His musical talent was honed from a very early age with lessons from his father before been
nurtured at the CMS Grammar School where he eventually became the School Choral director and
pianist. His debut was at the age of seven when He performed at the Economic Community of West
African States (ECOWAS) conference concert at the Sheraton Hotels in Abuja with the group Rising
Star led by Tosin Adejumo and he has since then been involved in several educational
and performance tours both in and outside the country.

Ayo Ajayi is the Creative Director of Declassical Arts & Entertainment, a top production company
in Nigeria which has produced phenomenal musical theatre performances like OMG the Musical,
Legends the Musical, The Lost Chord, Fela; Arrest the Music, Gula’, Ajala, Home the Musical, Ununcha the
musical, to mention but a few. He was also the Music Director for Midnight Hotel, Wait, Evolution of African
Music, Full Circle, 'Obele and The Storyteller'. Ayo, who has taught music in schools for more than two
decades is the immediate past President of the MUSON School Alumni Association and
is also a Pianist, Organist, Composer, and Choral Conductor.
